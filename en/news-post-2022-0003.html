Nanya Suppliers Join Earth Hour Initiative

May

Climate Change is a global issue and we are at a defining moment. As global warming has become more and more severe, resulting in climate anomalies such as sudden rainstorms, forest fires, droughts, and power shortages. Many industries have been affected to varying degrees. Without drastic action today, adapting to these impacts in the future will be more difficult and costly.

To protect our planet, Nanya has called out to our suppliers to join this advocacy project, Earth Hour, and 54 corporates responded. Earth Hour is an annual global campaign to show support for environmentally sustainable actions to combat climate change. Nanya has been supporting this event since 2018 and calls out to our suppliers to join us. It is a call to take responsibility for our environmental impact and join a growing international community who are committed to leading global environmental change.

Nanya continues putting efforts into sustainable development and environmental protection. This year we announced a 10-year renewable energy purchase agreement that will supply 250 million kWh of solar energy in total. A step forward to our goal of Net Zero emission.

Nanya will continue to act green and work hand in hand with suppliers to drive a sustainable supply chain for a better future.

* Earth Hour was started in 2007 by WWF and partners as a symbolic lights-out event in Sydney to raise awareness of climate change, held every year on the last Saturday of March. Earth Hour engages supporters in more than 190 countries and territories, all taking action to ensure a brighter future for people and the planet. Earth Hour is now one of the world's largest grassroots movements for the environment. Today, Earth Hour aims to increase awareness and spark global conversations on protecting nature, tackling the climate crisis, and working together to shape a brighter future for us all.
